Join the Memory Lab Network

In August of 2018, IMLS granted DCPL two more years of funding to add seven more partners to the Memory Lab Network. The application to apply for our second cohort will be opened in the Summer of 2019. Organizations will be required to submit a statement of interest and identify project leads who will build the local Memory Lab digitization stations and programs, attend in-person and virtual training, and contribute to project documentation.
